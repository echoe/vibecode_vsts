A six oscillator synthesizer that you can swap any of the oscillators into filters with.


Synth options:


-Oscillator types: Sine, Triangle, Saw, Pulse, Additive(just another wave)
-Filter types: Lowpass, Highpass, Bipole, Comb
-Three simple effects; Delay, Reverb, Chorus
-You can sync an oscillator to DAW tempo and use it as an LFO. Do you want more LFOs? Why have more LFOs when you can just have more oscillators?
-You can modulate any oscillator with any other oscillator.
-You can pass audio into an oscillator with the audio routing matrix.
-Preset support.
-Incredibly simple randomization.
-An Init button to avoid the randomization.
