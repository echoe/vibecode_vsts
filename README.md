Synths:
2oscfm: a 2 oscillator fm synth. the simple version of six sounds.
6oscfm: a 6 oscillator fm synth. a scaled up version of 2oscfm.

six sounds: kind of my dream VST - a 6 oscillator, 6 ADSR, fm synth with a mod matrix and audio routing matrix, where each oscillator can be a filter. this is what the 2osc and 6osc synth were leading to.

And probably actually designing the additive part of it, I think it would be cool to have something that lets you get a ton of different additive sounds with only a few controls. I will probably rename it to something better that reflects what it is as well. six sounds reflects some of the inspiration (opsix, six sines) but I think it's enough of its own thing at this point.

I think I'm going to change the name. That, adding a granular filter, implementing additive, and making the modulation work are the remaining things.
